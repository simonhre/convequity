Three-Layer Risk Mitigation
While growth equities can deliver high compounded returns, they come with volatility and drawdowns. We employ three sophisticated strategies to mitigate this profile:
Quantitative Risk Model
Proprietary valuation scoring incorporating DCF projections, Rule of X, forward growth and margin curves, and implied volatility analysis
Value Factor Integration
Tactical positions in value tech stocks to manage growth factor exposure and generate lower volatility alpha during transitions
Reverse VC Strategy
Up to 10% allocated to shorting overvalued companies with limited growth, weak leadership, and no architectural moat
Monetizing Overvaluation
When narratives diverge from fundamentals, we use targeted shorts and put options to mitigate downside and create alpha during corrections
